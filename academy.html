📝 Daily Practice Drills

One prompt per day. Record it, even if you don't post it.

D1 Record yourself saying "Hey, it's [your name]" three different ways. Which feels most natural?
D2 Talk about what you had for breakfast for 30 seconds without stopping.
D3 Share one thing you're good at and why. No disclaimers allowed.
D4 Record a take, watch it back, then record the same thing again. Notice the difference.
D5 Give advice to someone just starting in your field. Be direct.
D6 Tell a quick story from your week. Beginning, middle, end. Under 60 seconds.
D7 Record your best hook from this week. Then record it with 20% more energy.
